Spotlight on Your Experience

We support all individuals no matter their religion, race, ethnicity, age, gender identity, gender expression, or sexual orientation.

Our campuses are fully accessible with designated parking, electronic doors, elevators, specifically equipped fountains and washrooms, and fully accessible units in residence.

We also have a unique Light Therapy Lounge, equipped with specialty daylight spectrum lighting to help combat seasonal affective disorder and general lack of energy depression.
